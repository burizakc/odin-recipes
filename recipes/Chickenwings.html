Chicken Wings!

Description

Chicken wings are a favorite gameday snack

Some of the greatest flavors come from a well fried wing and some awesome sauce.

Ingredients

  • Chicken wings
  • Sauce of your choice
  • Oil for frying

Steps

  1. Pat chicken wings dry with a paper towel
  2. Drop chicken wings in boiling oil
  3. Fry for 3-5 minutes depending on crispiness desire.
  4. Carefully place cooked wings in a bowl
  5. Pour sauce into bowl and toss to coat
  6. plate and serve
